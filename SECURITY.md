# Security Policies

## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vunerability within the Bot, please send an email directly to `toby@tobezdev.com` with information about what you have found.
The email should include the following information at the very least, usually more:
- How to reproduce the vunerability;
- What is affected by the vunerability (i.e: is user data leaked? are config keys leaked? etc);
- Screenshots or a screen recording of you producing the vunerability;
- Any other information you think we may need.
All vunerabilities, if found to be legitimate, will see an immediate* fix with changes being deployed as soon as possible to reduce the impact on members.
> *The issue will be looked into immediately, but a fix may take a few hours to complete and deploy to remove the vunerability entirely. Appropriate actions will be taken in order to minimise the impact of the vunerability immediately, but complete removal of it may take some time.
## Entitlement to Reward

If you report a vunerability to the team, you may be entitied to a reward, whether monetary or of other form, entirely at the discretion of the maintainers of this repository.
Some criteria you need to meet for a reward are as follows:
- It must be a new, unreported vunerability;
- It must have an affect on users of the bot, rather than just being a small bug;
- It must be reproducable within your local applicable law;
- It must be a flaw of our product, rather than an external cause (i.e: the Discord or Audius APIs).

This is not a full list of requirements, and the descision to administer rewards lies soley with the maintainers of this repository.

## Contact

For any further question on our security policies, please send an email to `toby@tobezdev.com`. We thank you for bringing these vunerabilities to our attention and for helping to protect our users. 💜
